Choose your destination Sea, mountains, cities, national parks in Saudi Arabia every destination is a unique experience to be fully enjoyed!

Alula

Maraya

Maraya Alula, a stunning mirrored structure in AlUla, Saudi Arabia, seamlessly integrates modern design with the desert landscape. This architectural gem hosts cultural events and exhibitions while embodying sustainability. It symbolizes AlUla's rich heritage and invites visitors to experience the desert's timeless beauty.

Riyadh

Wonder Garden

Wonder Garden, a Riyadh Season zone, it is made up of three different zones where you can go on unique rides , An amusement park that will take you through a whole imaginative and whimsical experience.

Alkhobar

Ithra’s Library

Ithra’s Library one of the largest public libraries in the Dhahran, A dynamic and unique place for reinforcing culture spreading knowledge and encouraging inter-cultural communication

Jeddah

Bayada Island

Bayada Island, nestled in the Pacific Ocean, boasts pristine beaches and lush greenery. Its crystalline waters teem with marine life, offering excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities. Visitors are enchanted by the island's unspoile d charm and vibrant coral reefs. Bayada Island is a serene paradise for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

Buraydah

Dates City

The city hosts vibrant date festivals and markets, showcasing the rich diversity of date culture. Visitors can immerse themselves in the aroma and taste of this iconic fruit while exploring Buraydah's unique heritage.

ABOUT US

We were born to explore!

Tourism development is an important driver of growth for the future of Saudi Arabia. It is one of the key pillars at the heart of Vision 2030’s plan to help diversify the economy and reduce reliance on oil. The Ministry of Tourism, the Saudi Tourism Authority and the Tourism Development Fund were established in line with international best practice and with clear remits to support the growth of this significant sector and help it to flourish.
